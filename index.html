CentR

Redifing Control

Bringing all home appliance control
for doing stuff with your phone

Centr is a single platform device that serves as a mid point for all your devices. Center is a voice operated product that can do google searches, alert you to notifications and calls, and allow you to take calls directly from Centr. In addition Centr can control any WiFi or bluetooth enabled devices like Smart TVs or Smart Coffee Makers.

Use Case

Lets say you wake up and you want a coffee. You simply tell Centr to make your coffee, and Center will then send the message to your smart coffee maker. Or let's say you arrive home late and you just want to relax. You can say, "Turn on tv and play family guy." Center will do that along with locations of the connected bluthooth devices. So if you get home and it's dark Centr will turn on the lights in the room as you enter.
